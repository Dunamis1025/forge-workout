# FORGE — AI Workout Tracker

> *Built by someone who wanted Brad Pitt's physique but couldn't afford a personal trainer.*

---

## The Origin Story

I'm an IT student studying cybersecurity — not software engineering. Building apps was never part of the plan.

It started with a simple frustration: I wanted a physique like Brad Pitt in Troy. But he's a Hollywood actor with a dedicated trainer, chef, and years of preparation. I'm a regular Asian guy with a completely different body structure and a student budget.

So I started uploading photos of my physique to ChatGPT and asking for customized workout programs. The results were actually solid. But typing out detailed prompts every single time — explaining my goals, my body type, my current stats — became a tedious chore.

That frustration became this app.

---

## What FORGE Does

FORGE is an AI-powered workout tracker that replaces the repetitive prompt-writing with a clean, reusable interface.

- **AI Program Generator** — Describe your goal physique in plain English (e.g. *"Brad Pitt in Troy"* or *"Angelina Jolie in Tomb Raider"*), optionally upload a photo of your current physique, and get a tailored 4-day workout program generated by AI
- **Workout Logger** — Log your weight, sets, and reps for each exercise every session
- **Progressive Overload Tracker** — See exactly how much stronger you've gotten week over week, with visual progress bars and trend charts
